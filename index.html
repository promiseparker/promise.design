About Me

Design is a
conversation.

I'm a product designer from Nigeria, focused on building experiences that feel effortless and earn trust. I work at the intersection of user research, systems thinking, and visual craft — always anchored in what real people actually need.

I've shaped products across industries — starting at ConnectNigeria, levelling up at Leftlane Technologies, then going on to design multi-sided marketplaces at OrdaEats and consumer platforms at Keenzchillz. Those four companies taught me how to design for diverse users, tight constraints, and real-world impact. Currently open to full-time roles and select freelance projects.
